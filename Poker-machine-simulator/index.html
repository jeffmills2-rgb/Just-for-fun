Auto speed fast

Every result comes from Math.random(). The visible 5 × 3 reel grid is the actual outcome: active paylines are evaluated from left to right and Hold & Spin is scatter-triggered. The RTP slider scales the payout table and feature values so you can compare a 60%, 80%, 90% or 99% machine over many simulated plays. The Spin Until Feature button rapidly autospins until a 6+ orb trigger appears, then pauses to open the feature screen. The feature is an educational Hold & Spin model: triggering orbs lock into their same positions, prize balls drop into the grid, each new orb resets respins to 3, and a full 15-orb screen awards the Grand. The gamble game is a fair side bet: red/black pays ×2, and the exact suit pays ×4.

Educational simulation only — not a gambling product, no wagering, no payouts. Shortcuts: Space spin · A auto · Esc stop.
